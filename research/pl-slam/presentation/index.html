Line-Based Map Initialization


Assumption:
$R_1=R^T$, $R_2=I$, $R_3=R$

The cross product of two vectors $\textbf{l}$ will be parallel to the line $PQ$ and at the same time orthogonal to the third vector:

$\textbf{l}_2^T (R^T\textbf{l}_1 \times R\textbf{l}_3) = 0$

For small rotations:

$R = \begin{bmatrix}1 & -r_3 & r_2 \\ r_3 & 1 & -r_1 \\ -r_2 & r_1 & 1\end{bmatrix}$

Evaluate eight possible solutions and keep the one that minimizes equation 1

Line-Based Optimization

Projection Error

$E_p(\textbf{P},\textbf{Q},\textbf{l},\theta,\textbf{K}) = \textbf{P}-\pi(\textbf{P},\theta,\textbf{K})$

$E_l(\textbf{P},\textbf{Q},\textbf{l},\theta,\textbf{K}) = E_{pl}^2(\textbf{P},\textbf{l},\theta,\textbf{K}) + E_{pl}^2(\textbf{Q},\textbf{l},\theta,\textbf{K})$
        with $E_l(\textbf{P},\textbf{l},\theta,\textbf{K}) = l^T\pi(\textbf{P},\theta,\textbf{K})$


Bundle Adjustment

$C = \sum_{i,j} \rho (\textbf{E}_{i,j}^T \Omega_{i,j}^{-1} \textbf{E}_{i,j} + \textbf{E}_{i,j}^{\prime T} \Omega_{i,j}^{\prime -1} \textbf{E}_{i,j}^{\prime} + \textbf{E}_{i,j}^{\prime\prime T} \Omega_{i,j}^{\prime\prime -1} \textbf{E}_{i,j}^{\prime\prime} )$

$C = \sum_{i,j} \rho (\textbf{E}_{i,j}^T \Omega_{i,j}^{-1} \textbf{E}_{i,j})$

Line-Based Relocalization: EPnPL

$\arg\!\min_{\mu} \left \{ \left \| M_{p}\mu \right \|^2 + E_l(\left \{\textbf{P}^i\right \},\left \{\textbf{Q}^i\right \},\left \{\textbf{l}^i\right \},\theta,\left \{\textbf{K}^i\right \}) \right \} $
